# Practical Information

## Summer School Venue

The AI for Nuclear Thermal Hydraulics Summer School will take place in:

**Atrium Area, B Block**  
STFC Daresbury Laboratory  
Keckwick Lane  
Daresbury  
Warrington  
WA4 4AD

Please follow the local signs when you arrive. Registration details will be provided closer to the event.

---

## Accommodation

Accommodation is available for participants who requested it during registration.

**Hotel:**  
The Park Royal  
Stretton Road  
Warrington  
WA4 4NS  
Website: [theparkroyal.co.uk](https://www.theparkroyal.co.uk/)

Accommodation for registered participants who requested it has been pre-paid by CCP-NTH. Breakfast is included.

---

## Lunch

Lunch vouchers will be provided each day. These vouchers can be used in the Daresbury Laboratory restaurants.

Reserved seating will be available for summer school participants.

---

## Travel Between Daresbury Laboratory and the Hotel

Participants are encouraged to share cars where possible when travelling between Daresbury Laboratory and The Park Royal hotel.

If you require a taxi, please add your name to the taxi request sheet by 12:00 each day. The organising and administrative team will help arrange shared taxis where possible.

---

## Computing Requirements

Participants should bring a laptop and charger.

Please complete the software setup before arriving at the summer school. The environment setup guide is available here:

[Environment Setup](https://github.com/CCP-NTH/ai4nth-summer-school/blob/main/environment/README.md)

Participants are encouraged to run the environment check notebook before the event.

---

## Before You Arrive

Please make sure you have:

- confirmed your registration;
- informed the organisers of any accommodation or dietary requirements;
- installed the software environment;
- run the environment check notebook;
- brought your laptop and charger.
