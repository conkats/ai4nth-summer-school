# Teaching Materials

Teaching materials will be added closer to the AI for Nuclear Thermal Hydraulics Summer School.

The repository is structured to support reusable, reproducible course delivery. Materials are organised by day and include space for slides, notebooks, exercises, solutions, datasets and environment setup.

## Repository Structure

- `environment/` — Python/Jupyter environment setup, including conda, pip and Docker starter files.
- `datasets/` — dataset documentation, metadata, raw and processed data locations, split files and examples.
- `course_materials/` — slides, notebooks, exercises and solutions organised by teaching day.
- `instructors/` — checklists and templates for lecturer submissions and practical-session planning.

## Course Materials by Day

- `course_materials/day0_onboarding/`
- `course_materials/day1_foundations/`
- `course_materials/day2_tree_models/`
- `course_materials/day3_surrogates_gp/`
- `course_materials/day4_neural_networks/`
- `course_materials/day5_vvuq_optimisation/`

Released notebooks, slides, PDFs and HTML tools are placed directly in the relevant day folder. Exercise and solution subfolders are available where a session needs separate practical tasks or reference outputs.

Materials remain subject to lecturer content checks and release permissions.
